Key Visual Enhancements

🎯 Affordability Indicator

  • Colored dot next to cost (green/yellow/red)
  • Subtle card border glow for affordable items
  • Opacity reduction for unaffordable cards

📊 Energy Meter

  • Thin progress bar at card bottom
  • Shows % of required energy you have
  • Color-coded: green (100%), yellow (80%+), red (<80%)

🎮 Smart Buttons

  • Dynamic text based on affordability
  • Color matches affordability state
  • Disabled state for unaffordable items

💡 Hover Details

  • Cost badge expands on hover
  • Shows current resources vs. cost
  • Calculates what you'll have left

✨ Bonus Indicators

  • Small dots for active bonuses
  • Quest progress badges
  • Tooltips explain modifiers

🎨 Minimal Impact

  • Preserves current card design
  • Adds functionality without clutter
  • Progressive disclosure of details
